About Jonathan Middlemiss

Jonathan Middlemiss was a renowned artist known for his exceptional skills in creating intricate ceramic sculptures which integrates themes of geometry, ecology, and landscape. His art is inspired by nature, and he often used natural materials such as wood, stone, and metal in his works. He was born in 1949 and passed away in 2021, leaving behind an impressive collection of art that continues to inspire and captivate art enthusiasts worldwide.

His daughter, Serena Rosaria, it committed to preserving her father's artistic legacy. With a background in art history and a deep understanding of her father's work, Serena is the ideal person to organize exhibitions, secure commissions, and promote her father's artwork to a wider audience. She has also been instrumental in cataloging and documenting her father's extensive collection of art, ensuring that it is preserved and accessible to future generations.

Serena's extensive knowledge of her father's work has allowed her to continue his legacy in a meaningful way. She understands the themes and techniques that her father used, and she has a keen eye for identifying his artwork. In addition to her knowledge of her father's work, Serena also has a wealth of experience in the art world. Jonathan Middlemiss was a talented artist who left behind a remarkable legacy. Thanks to his daughter Serena's efforts, his art continues to inspire and captivate audiences worldwide.
